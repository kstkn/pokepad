# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2024

## Data Collection

Pokepad does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. The application operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device.

## Local Data Storage

Pokepad uses your browser's localStorage API to store:
- Audio file paths you select
- Tab configurations and organization
- Cue points for audio files
- Custom card names

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

## Network Activity

Pokepad does not make any network requests. The application:
- Does not connect to the internet
- Does not send analytics or telemetry
- Does not check for updates automatically
- Does not communicate with any external services

## Third-Party Components

- **Electron**: Desktop application framework. Electron itself does not collect user data in this application.
- **electron-builder**: Build tool used only during development/build process, does not affect runtime behavior.

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- All data is stored locally and can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing browser storage
- No data is shared with third parties
- No data is used for advertising or marketing purposes

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
